About the Project

As knowledge workers, we consume and produce vast amounts of information daily, but fail to utilize that knowledge when we need it most.

Lessons from past projects, insights from books, and ideas from meetings are all somewhere, but unfindable. While existing AI tools like ChatGPT help with individual tasks, the overall workflow remains manual.

We propose an automated approach where anyone can leverage Agentic AI to transform information into knowledge and apply that knowledge to achieve their goals. The productivity revolution that developers have already experienced can now be applied to all knowledge workâ€”planning, data analysis, and beyond. By combining Obsidian, Claude Code, automated workflow tools, and a desktop app, you can experience extraordinary productivity.

This guide covers Personal Knowledge Management in the AI era (AI4PKM) step-by-step, from theory to practice.
