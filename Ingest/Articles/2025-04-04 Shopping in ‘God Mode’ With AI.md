## Summary
Cher's digital closet was just the beginning. Online shopping is becoming intelligent, predictive, and visually intuitive.

## Highlights
OpenAI is starting to treat shopping as a dedicated use case, adding tools like Operator, which lets users ask for product recommendations, and Deep Research, which aggregates specs, reviews, and price data for side-by-side comparisons. Meanwhile, startups are building vertically integrated solutions for every step in the stack, from product discovery and smarter, multimodal search to virtual try-ons and autonomous post-purchase support.
