## Summary
Google's True Moonshot discusses Google's history and challenges in the field of artificial intelligence and machine learning. The article highlights the company's go-to-market gap and business model problem when it comes to assistants, as well as its legal challenges with Android. The author also mentions Google's advantages in terms of data and infrastructure, as well as its potential to integrate AI models into its products. The article concludes by discussing Google's foray into hardware and the launch of its Pixel phone, which includes the exclusive Pixie AI assistant.
