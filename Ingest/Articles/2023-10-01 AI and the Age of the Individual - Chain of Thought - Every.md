## Summary
Recent advances in AI will empower individuals to make an impact on a scale matching large businesses, research labs, and creative organizations. AI pushes the cost of intelligence toward zero, opening up new domains of achievement previously unavailable to individuals and small teams. For writers, AI tools can assist with research, fact-checking, and content creation in various formats such as videos and podcasts. Similarly, founders of startups can benefit from AI tools that automate repetitive tasks and reduce the need for large teams. While these technological shifts have the potential to create more impactful work, they also require policy and regulation to address the changing job landscape.
