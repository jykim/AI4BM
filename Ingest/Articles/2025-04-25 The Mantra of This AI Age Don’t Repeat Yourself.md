## Summary
Language models handle the repetition—people decide what matters

## Highlights
This shift in how we see the world aligns with what I've previously called the [allocation economy](https://every.to/chain-of-thought/the-knowledge-economy-is-over-welcome-to-the-allocation-economy?sid=56637). As AI takes over these repetitive tasks, our role changes from doing the work ourselves, to deciding what work needs to be done and how to best allocate our resources to do it.
In the allocation economy, the key skill becomes knowing how to effectively leverage AI to handle these repetitive elements, freeing us up for more creative and strategic thinking.
So, if you’re wondering how your world might change over the next few years, do a little experiment today. Try to notice all of the ways you’re repeating yourself. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jspdqp33tmdkhvfb1n74zva0))
