## Summary
Google CEO Sundar Pichai discusses the release of Gemini, Google's next-generation AI model. Gemini is a multimodal model that combines text and other forms of data to improve capabilities and contribute to the progress of the field. Pichai emphasizes the potential for AI to continue advancing and the importance of developing safety techniques and responsible deployment. He also discusses the need to make AI technology widely accessible and address societal challenges such as inequality. Pichai acknowledges the duality of AI's impact and the need for frameworks and legal frameworks to navigate issues such as intellectual property and fair use.
