## Summary
In an interview with WIRED, Google CEO Sundar Pichai discusses the company's focus on AI and its recent struggles with competition. Pichai mentions the company's shift to being AI-first and the responsibility that comes with maintaining user trust. He also addresses concerns about the impact of AI on Google's search engine business model and the company's ongoing antitrust case. Pichai emphasizes the importance of innovation and collaboration in the semiconductor industry, and his confidence in Google's ability to continue working closely with NVIDIA.
