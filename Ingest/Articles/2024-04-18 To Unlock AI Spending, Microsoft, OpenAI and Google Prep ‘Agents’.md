## Summary
Microsoft, OpenAI, and Google are enhancing AI features to handle complex tasks with minimal guidance. These "agents" aim to boost AI spending by automating actions like creating invoices and rewriting code. Companies are working on agents that can perform tasks like transferring data and booking travel accommodations.

## Highlights
OpenAI, Google and Facebook owner Meta Platforms are each developing their own versions of agents. It’s part of the industry’s broader effort to turn the excitement ChatGPT sparked 18 months ago into recurring revenue for a slew of companies that sell such technology.
